Chamber One · The TATA Legacy Room
🏛️
The House of TATA
Every Tanishq piece carries the trust of a 150-year-old legacy.

The first chamber is lined with portraits and headlines spanning over a century. At its centre stands a brass plaque: "Founded by Jamshedji Nusserwanji Tata. Headquartered at Bombay House."

TATA Group is one of India's most respected conglomerates — spanning 30 listed companies, 10 verticals, operating in 100+ countries across 6 continents. Every Tanishq associate is part of this extraordinary legacy.

The Tata Code of Conduct (TCOC) — a set of ethical principles — governs every Tata company, including Tanishq. It is the foundation of the trust customers place in us.

Chamber Two · The Founders Gallery
✨
The Birth of Tanishq
A name, a vision, and a jeweller who changed India forever.

The second chamber is the Founders Gallery — a warm room lined with the story of how Tanishq came to be.

The name Tanishq was coined by Mr. Xerxes Desai — Founder — by marrying two words: "Tan" (body) and "Nishk" (a gold ornament). Another reading: "Ta" for Tata and "Nishq" for gold coin.

What began as a venture into 18K gold watches studded with precious stones grew into India's largest jewellery brand — a 22K gold jeweller who understood the evolving needs of the Indian woman.

🎙 Mr. Xerxes Desai"Tanishq is a name synonymous with superior craftsmanship, exclusive designs, and superlative product quality."
